First Open Call — Free to Apply

An open call for contemporary painting & graphic art.

Our first curated digital exhibition is open for submissions — completely free, reviewed individually, looking for artists across different visual styles, anywhere in the world.

Apply now
About this open call

Ruri Gallery is a new international platform for contemporary painting and graphic art. With this first call, we want to bring together artists working across different visual styles and traditions — not a single house look. We're not promising a permanent profile to everyone who applies. What we are committing to: a PDF catalog of this first call, distributed to help grow international awareness of contemporary painting and graphic art — and a profile on Ruri Gallery for the artists selected for the exhibition.

“This first call is free because trust should be earned, not charged for.”
What we look for

Contemporary painting or graphic art — any visual style, technique, or scale.

A consistent, finished body of work — not a single image.

Any career stage, anywhere in the world.

What you receive

A profile on Ruri Gallery, if selected for our debut exhibition.

Inclusion in our debut PDF catalog — distributed to grow international awareness of contemporary painting and graphic art.

No entry fee — we're earning your trust, not charging for it.
